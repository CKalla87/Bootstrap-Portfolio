About Me



My name is Christopher Anthony Kalla. I have always been curious how applications work ever since I purchased my first smartphone years ago. Even before that I was learning how to use programs for audio recording such as Pro Tools, Logic and Reason. The reason why I wanted to become a Software Engineer is because of my passion for being creative while helping others in the process. I believe I can accomplish this being a Software Engineer.

I plan on continuing my journey in Software Development by getting my Masters in Software Engineering and working full time building applications. I also plan to design audio applications and websites in my spare time.
